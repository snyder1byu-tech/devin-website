The Saga

A swashbuckling franchise spanning five films, the Pirates of the Caribbean series follows the eccentric Captain Jack Sparrow and his encounters with cursed treasure, mythical seas, and the immortal dead.

The Legend

From the cursed Aztec gold of the Black Pearl to the heart of Davy Jones, each film weaves together nautical myth, dark magic, and a rogue's gallery of unforgettable scoundrels and heroes.

"Me? I'm dishonest. And a dishonest man you can always trust to be dishonest. Honestly, it's the honest ones you want to watch out for."
— Captain Jack Sparrow

The World

Set in the golden age of Caribbean piracy, the films blend the historical with the fantastical — from Port Royal's rigid society to the mysterious Davy Jones's Locker at the edge of the world.

The Music

Hans Zimmer's iconic score, featuring "He's a Pirate" and "Davy Jones," became one of film history's most beloved soundtracks — instantly recognizable and endlessly stirring.

"Not all treasure is silver and gold, mate."
— Captain Jack Sparrow

The Five Voyages

I

The Curse of the Black Pearl

2003 · Directed by Gore Verbinski

Blacksmith Will Turner teams with the eccentric pirate Jack Sparrow to rescue his love, Elizabeth Swann, from the cursed crew of the Black Pearl — undead pirates doomed by stolen Aztec gold.

II

Dead Man's Chest

2006 · Directed by Gore Verbinski

Jack owes a blood debt to the terrifying Davy Jones, ruler of the ocean depths. To escape his fate, he must find the Dead Man's Chest — and the still-beating heart within it.

III

At World's End

2007 · Directed by Gore Verbinski

Will, Elizabeth, and Barbossa journey to World's End to rescue Jack from Davy Jones's Locker. The Pirate Lords of the Brethren Court unite against the East India Trading Company.

IV

On Stranger Tides

2011 · Directed by Rob Marshall

Jack crosses paths with the enigmatic Angelica and the brutal Blackbeard as they race to find the legendary Fountain of Youth, whose waters grant immortality at a terrible price.

V

Dead Men Tell No Tales

2017 · Directed by Joachim Rønning & Espen Sandberg

The fearsome ghost-captain Armando Salazar escapes the Devil's Triangle to hunt down Jack Sparrow. Young Henry Turner seeks the Trident of Poseidon to break all curses of the sea.

Rogues & Heroes

Captain Jack Sparrow

The eccentric, unpredictable captain of the Black Pearl. His methods are unorthodox and his motives inscrutable, yet he always seems to end up exactly where he intends.

⚔️

A gifted blacksmith and skilled swordsman, Will is the son of Bootstrap Bill Turner. His selfless courage drives him to risk everything for those he loves.

👑
Elizabeth Swann
Keira Knightley

The governor's daughter turned Pirate King. Elizabeth's intelligence, bravery, and moral conviction make her one of the most formidable figures in the Brethren Court.

💀
Davy Jones
Bill Nighy

Ruler of the ocean depths, cursed to command the Flying Dutchman. His tentacled face and pipe organ are iconic — as is the heart he locked away to forget his lost love.

🦜
Captain Barbossa
Geoffrey Rush

A cunning, ruthless, and oddly honorable pirate. Barbossa serves as rival and reluctant ally to Jack — their dynamic is one of the franchise's most entertaining threads.

⚓
Bootstrap Bill Turner
Stellan Skarsgård

Will's father, cursed to serve aboard the Flying Dutchman. His sacrifice and honor echo across generations — and across the deep, cold sea.

Myths of the Deep

The Brethren Court

Nine Pirate Lords rule the nine seas, each holding a piece of eight — in truth, ordinary trinkets imbued with symbolic power. Together they form the Brethren Court, the highest council of piracy, capable of convening to face existential threats to the pirate way of life.

Cursed Treasures

Aztec Gold Any crew that spends a coin from the chest of Cortés is cursed to undeath — unable to feel, taste, or live — until every coin is returned with a blood offering.
Heart of Davy Jones Jones removed his own heart and locked it in a chest to spare himself grief. Whoever controls the heart controls Jones — and through him, the seas.
Trident of Poseidon The mythical weapon of the sea god. It binds all curses of the ocean and can only be shattered by a true Turner — born of the sea.
Fountain of Youth Hidden in uncharted waters, the Fountain grants immortality but demands a price — one life must be given so another may drink.

The Kraken

Davy Jones's monstrous leviathan, summoned from the inky deep to drag ships and men to the locker. Its tentacles can crush the largest warship in seconds. To be marked by the Black Spot is to draw its eye — and seal your fate.

Ships of Legend

The Black Pearl The fastest ship on the seven seas, with sails as dark as night. Once lost, it can be called back from the depths — for a price only Jack is willing to pay.
The Flying Dutchman Cursed vessel of Davy Jones, crewed by souls of the drowned. It can sail beneath the waves and emerge from anywhere — silent, inevitable, and merciless.
The Queen Anne's Revenge Blackbeard's fearsome flagship. Rigged with the captured sails of a hundred prizes and armed with flame projectors, she is terror itself given a hull and keel.
The Silent Mary The ghost ship of Captain Salazar, crewed by the undead. She sails with her bow eternally broken open — a wound that never healed, like the men inside her.
